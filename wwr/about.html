History

Founded in 2022, Asseka Site Rafting was born from a passion for adventure and a deep appreciation for the natural beauty of our rivers. Our founder, Aristid Wilbrech ELENGA-ASSEKA, an avid outdoors enthusiast and experienced rafting guide, recognized the need for a rafting company that not only provided thrilling experiences but also emphasized safety, environmental stewardship, and community engagement.
